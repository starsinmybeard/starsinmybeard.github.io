Diary Entry:

9/23/1969


'The Summer of Love'


They may have put a man on the moon this summer, but the real astronauts went to space in a little town called Woodstock, New York.


You know the vibes. The power was in our hands and the love was flowing. Garcia was on the 6 string, Hendrix made us love America again, and the only brown acid me and my pals had to worry about was a bad bleach job done on our levis. -Greg

'Leaving on a Jet Plane'

Well. Sometimes the man wins. I have started my work as a software consultant. After seeing my first paycheck, I have decided to renounce my hippie ways, and relocate out of Yellow Springs. I will be stepping down as moderator of the Hippie HQ. -GW
